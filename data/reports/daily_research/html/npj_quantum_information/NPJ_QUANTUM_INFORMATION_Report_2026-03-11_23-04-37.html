量子导引使得一方能够通过局域测量影响另一方的远程量子态。尽管导引是众多量子信息任务的基础，但现有文献中的检测方法主要局限于特定测量场景或低维系统。在本研究中，我们提出了一种用于导引检测的偏序格框架，该框架能够探索任意维度和任意测量设置下的导引现象。我们推导出了两比特态、高维Werner态和各向同性态的导引不等式，这些不等式设定了比现有结果更为严格的判定标准。值得注意的是，已知的高维结果实际上是新方法在某种意义上的近似极限。

Abstract

Quantum steering enables one party to influence another remote quantum state by local measurement. While steering is fundamental to many quantum information tasks, the existing detection methods in the literature are mainly constrained to either specific measurement scenario or low-dimensional systems. In this work, we propose a majorization lattice framework for steering detection, which is capable of exploring the steering in arbitrary dimension and measurement setting. Steering inequalities for two-qubit states, high-dimensional Werner states and isotropic states are obtained, which set even stringent bars than what has been reached yet. Notably, the known high-dimensional results turn out to be some kind of approximate limits of the new approach.

多色资源通过提升速率与可扩展性革新了经典通信网络；量子通信网络正面临类似的转折点。本文报道了一种基于光学频率梳实现的多色连续变量量子通信网络。我们研究了由多色量子网络构成的多模密度矩阵，并将有限模式隔离纳入安全性分析以界定窃听者可获取的信息量，进而计算网络总密钥率。研究表明，相较于其他复用技术，多色量子网络理论上可实现密钥率不随用户数量增加而下降的性能。实验中采用光学频率梳及双梳干涉探测技术构建了直传型与往返型量子通信网络，验证了高斯调制连续变量量子密钥分发协议，在均匀传输距离5公里时网络容量达19、总密钥率达8.75 Gbps（渐进情况），120公里时达0.82 Mbps（有限长效应），40公里时达89.10 Mbps（可组合安全性），40公里时达13.66 Mbps（可组合有限长安全性）。该实现不仅为高速多节点量子网络提供了技术支撑，也为未来连续变量量子互联网提供了解决方案。

Abstract

Polychromatic resources have transformed classical communication networks by boosting rate and scalability; quantum communication networks are reaching a similar inflection point. Here, we report a polychromatic continuous-variable quantum communication network enabled by optical frequency combs. The multi-mode density matrices constituted by polychromatic quantum networks are studied. We incorporate finite mode isolation into the security analysis to bound Eve’s accessible information and compute the total network secret key rate. It is demonstrated that, compared to other multiplexing techniques, polychromatic quantum networks can theoretically achieve a secret key rate without decreasing with the increase in users. In the experiment, direct-transmission type and round-trip type quantum communication networks were built using optical frequency combs and dual-comb interference detection technology. The Gaussian-modulated continuous-variable quantum key distribution (CV-QKD) protocol has been validated, with a network capacity of 19 and a total secret key rate of 8.75 Gbps at a uniform distance of 5 km (asymptotic case), 0.82 Mbps at 120 km (finite-size effect), 89.10 Mbps at 40 km (composable security), 13.66 Mbps at 40 km (composable finite-size security). This implementation not only provides technical support for a high-speed multi-node quantum network, but also provides a solution for the future quantum Internet with continuous variables.

摘要翻译

将基于固态发射器的单光子源与光子结构集成仍具挑战性，尤其是在实现与光纤兼容方面。这对于量子技术至关重要，因为单光子源是众多量子技术的基础构建模块。本研究展示了一种由胶体半导体纳米晶体构成的单光子源与通过离子交换技术制备的玻璃光波导（一种电信光子学中常用的光纤兼容平台）的集成。通过表面功能化和二氧化钛次级波导结构，我们成功将纳米发射器产生的单光子耦合至光波导中。实验证实单光子特性得以保持，并实现了1.2的珀塞尔因子。得益于二氧化钛次级波导层，耦合效率预计可超过25%，而仅将纳米晶体置于波导上方时耦合效率约为2%。该光波导通过尾纤与光纤连接，首次在室温下验证了基于玻璃光纤兼容平台的集成单光子源原理，其耦合效率提升了2.8倍。

Abstract

The integration of a single photon source based on a solid-state emitter with a photonic structure remains challenging and specifically when it comes to be compatible with optical fibres. It is important to do so for quantum technologies where single photon sources constitute building blocks for many technologies. In this work, we demonstrate the integration of a single photon source, made of a colloidal semiconductor nanocrystal, with a photonic structure made of a glass waveguide obtained by the ion-exchange technique, which is a fibre-compatible platform used in telecom photonics. Using surface functionalisation and a secondary waveguide of titanium dioxide, we manage to couple single photons from the nanoemitter to the optical waveguide. We observe the single photon character is preserved and demonstrates a 1.2 Purcell factor. A coupling efficiency over 25% is expected thanks to the titanium dioxide secondary waveguiding layer, compared to around 2% obtained for simply positioning the nanocrystal on top of the waveguide alone. The optical waveguide is then pigtailed to an optical fibre to demonstrate a first proof of principle of an integrated single photon source at room temperature with a fibre-compatible platform made of glass with a coupling efficiency increase of 2.8.
